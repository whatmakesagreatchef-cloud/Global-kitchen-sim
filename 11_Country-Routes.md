# Country Routes (Selectable)

## Design principle
Different routes teach different realities. None are “easy.” They are different.

## AU — Australia Route (Grit & Funding Hustle)
Pressures: time windows, travel/fundraising, industry mentor reliance  
Learns: resilience, planning, sponsorship reality, team culture  
Conflict style: mateship vs ego; direct confrontation

## JP — Japan Route (Precision & Standards)
Pressures: perfectionism, hierarchy, discipline fatigue  
Learns: mastery ladders, role clarity, process excellence  
Conflict style: tension builds quietly; big blow-ups if ignored

## FR — France Route (Classical Foundations & Critique)
Pressures: critique culture, tradition vs innovation, status/roles  
Learns: fundamentals, feedback resilience, brigade structure  
Conflict style: pride + hierarchy

## US — USA Route (Brand & Innovation)
Pressures: media expectations, big personalities, sponsorship optics  
Learns: pitching, innovation under time, leadership under spotlight  
Conflict style: loud early conflict; fast resolution options
